Introduction to Natural Language Processing

Taught by: Dragomir R. Radev, Ph.D.

Created by: University of Michigan

Offered at Coursera

Get Started

About this course: This course provides an introduction to the field of Natural Language Processing. It includes relevant background material in Linguistics, Mathematics, Probabilities, and Computer Science. Some of the topics covered in the class are Text Similarity, Part of Speech Tagging, Parsing, Semantics, Question Answering, Sentiment Analysis, and Text Summarization. The course includes quizzes, programming assignments in Python, and a final exam.
